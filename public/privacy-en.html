PickIt – Privacy Policy

Effective: July 11, 2026 · Version: 1.0

PickIt (the "App") is a shared family shopping list application. This policy explains what personal data we process, why, how long we keep it, and what rights you have.

1. Data controller

Name: Attila Juhász-Vedres
Email: jattila.np@gmail.com
App: PickIt (hu.pickit.app)

2. What data do we process?

Data Source Purpose
Email address Registration, account linking Sign-in, email verification, password reset
Password Registration Authentication (encrypted by Firebase Auth; we cannot read it)
Display name Provided by you Identification in your household, lists, and notifications
User ID (UID) Automatically generated Linking your account and data
Shopping lists, items, catalog You and household members Core app features, real-time sharing
Who checked what and when App usage Syncing list status across family members
Household name, invite code Provided by you Family sharing and joining
Push notification token With your permission Sending notifications about list changes
Language, font size preference Your choice Displaying the App according to your preferences
Crash / error reports Automatically when errors occur Fixing bugs and improving stability

You can use the App in guest mode without an email address. In that case, only a display name and technical identifier are stored.

3. Purpose and legal basis (GDPR)

  • Performance of a contract – providing App services (Art. 6(1)(b)).
  • Consent – push notifications (Art. 6(1)(a)); can be withdrawn in device or App settings.
  • Legitimate interest – collecting error reports to maintain reliability (Art. 6(1)(f)).

4. Storage location and retention

Account and list data are stored in Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication). Server-side processing primarily takes place in the europe-central2 (European) region.

We retain data for as long as your account and household data are needed to provide the service. You may request deletion of your personal data (see section 7).

The App temporarily caches list data on your device for offline use and faster loading.

5. Third parties (processors)

  • Google Firebase (Google Ireland Limited) – authentication, database, server-side functions. Privacy information
  • Expo / EAS (650 Industries, Inc.) – push notification delivery, app updates. Privacy policy
  • Sentry (Functional Software, Inc.) – crash and error reporting. Privacy policy

The App does not use advertising or marketing analytics SDKs and does not sell data to third parties.

6. Push notifications

If you enable them, we send notifications about list changes in your household (for example, who checked off an item). The device token required for notifications is linked to your Firebase profile. You can disable notifications in App settings or your device system settings.

7. Your rights

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • access your personal data;
  • request correction or deletion;
  • request restriction of processing;
  • object to certain processing;
  • data portability (where applicable);
  • lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority (in Hungary: NAIH).

Contact us at jattila.np@gmail.com for privacy requests. We will fulfill deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Children

The App is designed for family use but does not knowingly collect data from children under 16 without parental supervision. If you believe we processed a child's data without authorization, please contact us.

9. Security

Only signed-in, authorized users can access data. Access outside your household is restricted by server-side rules (Firestore Security Rules). Passwords are handled by Firebase Auth in encrypted form.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated in the App or on this page. The effective date is shown at the top.
